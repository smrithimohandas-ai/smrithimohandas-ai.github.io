The Story

Three companies. Two continents. Every time, a blank page.

I moved to the US for a Master’s in Human Resources and spent a decade building people functions at companies most people recognize. What I found is that the most meaningful work happens not when you’re running a mature people function, but when you’re the person standing in the room figuring out what needs to exist.

I’ve been that person at three very different companies. At Avaya, a 14,000-person global enterprise, I built programs during bankruptcy and back out of it. At Klaviyo, a hypergrowth startup racing toward IPO, I was the first Talent Management hire. At Freshworks, a post-IPO tech company, I’m designing the people infrastructure for 5,000+ people in Bengaluru.

In 2026 I made a deliberate choice to come back. Not because the US chapter was over, but because what’s being built in India’s tech ecosystem right now is genuinely exciting, and I wanted to be part of it with a decade of scale behind me.
